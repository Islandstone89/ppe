Kasteel De Berckt

As soon as you pass through the gate onto the estate, you feel a surprising sense of getting away from it all.

Stay at a real castle

Kasteel de Berckt, ‘The Castle’, has several different departments, with a couple of bedrooms and relax rooms for everyone. The friendly and flexible staff make your stay even more enjoyable. The centuries-old castle called ‘De Berckt’ is in a charming location alongside the river ‘Maas’. The property has an attractive courtyard and is surrounded by a moat and extensive grounds. Enjoy the tranquility of earlier times.

Your room

There are many different rooms for 2 people or more. There is a big chance you will share a room with other people, there is no guarantee we can book a single room for you.

Breakfast

Breakfast is included and will be served in the morning between 09.00 and 11.00 hrs.

Dinner Thursday

On Thursday evening a dinner will be served if you are interested, between 18.30 and 20.00 hrs. Dinner will cost € 17.50.
